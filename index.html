My education

With this background, I studied Biology at Colorado College and then pursued Population Biology for my Masters researching the Psittacine species of the South American continent and developing a plan for species' conservation. I have a second Masters in Geographical Information Systems at the University of Denver, and graciously worked as a partner between DU and the National Park Service.
